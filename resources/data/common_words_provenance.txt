Source: Peter Norvig's word frequency list
URL: http://norvig.com/ngrams/count_1w.txt
Companion page: http://norvig.com/spell-correct.html

Compiled by Peter Norvig (Director of Research, Google).
Derived from the Google Web Corpus — approximately 1 trillion words of English text
crawled from the public web. The original file contains ~333,000 unigrams with raw
frequency counts, sorted descending by frequency.

This file contains the top 5,000 words by frequency (first column of count_1w.txt),
one word per line, with the frequency counts stripped.

Downloaded: 2026-03-28
Extraction command: curl -s http://norvig.com/ngrams/count_1w.txt | head -5000 | awk '{print $1}'
